Got Questions?
WE HAVE ANSWERS
What do the different email access types mean?

Catchall accounts are Nike accounts made using your own custom domain, which, if set up properly, will forward all emails to one master email. Provided email accounts are where you supply us with the emails you would like the accounts to be made under, and you will have full access to those emails since you own them. No access accounts are accounts made with made-up Gmails, similar to other account providers, where you will have no access to the Gmail inboxes used unless you decide to try and make the accounts yourself.

What email access type should I pick?

Right now, we are seeing that Gmail or Outlook based accounts tend to have slightly higher success rates. Since password resets are so rare right now, our recommendation if you are on a budget is to go with no-access accounts. Catchall accounts are still hitting though, so if you do not have your own emails to use, that is still a solid second option.
